Frequently Asked Questions

What is Fountain Prime School?
Fountain Prime School is a co-educational institution located in Apa, Badagry West LCDA, Lagos State, offering comprehensive education from Nursery through Primary to Secondary levels. We are dedicated to nurturing young minds with academic excellence, moral values, and global awareness.
Our Motto
Dedication & Sound Knowledge
Our Vision
To be a leading educational institution recognized for fostering excellence in academics, innovation, and character. We aspire to cultivate well-rounded, confident, and socially responsible individuals equipped to excel in an ever-changing global society.
Our Mission
To provide holistic education that nurtures academic excellence, character development, and global citizenship. We create an inclusive, supportive environment where every student reaches their full potential and becomes a responsible, compassionate leader.
How do I apply for admission?
Visit our Student Portal to start your online application. Fill out the required forms, upload necessary documents, and submit. Our admissions team will contact you within 48 hours.
